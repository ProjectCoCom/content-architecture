# Chapter 4: Tier 2: Topic Cluster Strategy

### What This Is
Topic clusters are groups of in-depth articles that explore the subtopics introduced in your pillar content. Each cluster is dedicated to a specific theme and provides the detailed information that your audience is actively searching for. If a pillar is the book, clusters are the chapters.

### Why This Matters
Clusters are the engine of your content strategy. They allow you to target a wide range of medium-difficulty keywords, demonstrating your expertise across the full breadth of your core topic. A well-executed cluster strategy builds topical authority, which is a major ranking factor for search engines like Google.

### Topic Cluster Components

Every effective topic cluster should include:

1.  **A Core Article**: A comprehensive guide to the main cluster topic (2,500-4,000 words).
2.  **Supporting Articles**: 5-10 articles that dive deeper into specific aspects of the core topic.
3.  **Strategic Interlinking**: The core article should link up to the pillar and down to the supporting articles. Supporting articles should link back to the core article and to each other where relevant.
4.  **Clear Calls-to-Action**: Each article in the cluster should have a CTA that's relevant to its specific topic.
