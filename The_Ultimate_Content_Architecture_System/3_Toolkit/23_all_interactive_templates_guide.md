### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- **Getting bogged down in the details of the templates.** The templates are a guide, not a straightjacket. The goal is not to fill out every single box perfectly; it's to have a productive and strategic conversation with your team.
- **Not adapting the templates to your specific needs.** These templates are designed to be a starting point. Feel free to add, remove, or change the questions to make them more relevant to your business.
- **Forgetting that the templates are just a means to an end.** The goal is not to have a folder full of beautifully filled-out templates. The goal is to have a successful content marketing program. The templates are just a tool to help you to get there.
- **Not having a clear owner for each template.** To ensure that the templates are being used effectively and are being kept up-to-date, it's a good idea to assign a clear "owner" for each one.
