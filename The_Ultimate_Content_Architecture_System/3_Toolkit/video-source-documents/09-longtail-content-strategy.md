# Video Source for: Longtail Content Strategy

## Source Generation Prompts

1. How does a longtail content strategy directly connect content marketing efforts to business goals and customer acquisition?
2. What is the process for creating a formulaic, scalable approach to longtail content creation (e.g., [Action] + [Topic] + for + [Audience])?
3. Provide examples of how to brainstorm and build a matrix of variables for each component of a longtail content formula.
4. What is the ideal structure for a high-converting longtail landing page?
5. How can a business implement a programmatic or semi-programmatic approach to scale longtail content production from dozens to hundreds or thousands of pages?

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to develop a scalable longtail content strategy. The video must explain how to define a longtail content formula, brainstorm the variables for each component, and create a standardized, high-converting outline for a longtail landing page that is tailored to a specific audience and their unique challenge.
