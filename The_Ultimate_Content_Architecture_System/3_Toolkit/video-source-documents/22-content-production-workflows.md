# Video Source for: Content Production Workflows

## Source Generation Prompts

1. What are the key stages of a standardized, scalable content production workflow?
2. How can a team use a simple project management tool like Trello to manage their content workflow from ideation to promotion?
3. What is a "Content Brief Template" and why is it the most important document in the entire production workflow?
4. What are the benefits of automating parts of the content workflow, such as task assignments and status updates?
5. How do you conduct a "workflow retrospective" to identify bottlenecks and continuously improve your content production process?

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to build a smooth and efficient content production workflow. The video must provide a clear, 7-step workflow, show how to set up a visual tracking system using a tool like Trello, and emphasize the importance of a detailed content brief as the foundation of a scalable content operation.
