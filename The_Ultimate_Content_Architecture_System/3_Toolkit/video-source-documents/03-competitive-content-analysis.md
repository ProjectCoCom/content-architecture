# Video Source for: Competitive Content Analysis

## Source Generation Prompts

1. What are the key steps in conducting a comprehensive competitive content analysis?
2. How can you identify strategic content gaps and "white space" opportunities in a crowded market?
3. What are the best tools (both free and paid) for analyzing a competitor's content performance and SEO strategy?
4. Provide a framework for evaluating the quality and effectiveness of a competitor's content, beyond just the topics they cover.
5. Show examples of how a "content gap" analysis can lead to the creation of a unique and differentiated content strategy.

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that teaches viewers how to perform a strategic competitive content analysis. The video must provide a clear, 4-step process: identifying the competitive set, conducting a topic coverage audit, identifying content gaps, and performing a basic SEO competitive analysis.
