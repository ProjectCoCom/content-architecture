# Video Source for: Understanding Your Audience Architecture

## Source Generation Prompts

1. What is the difference between a traditional buyer persona and a more comprehensive audience architecture?
2. How does a deep understanding of audience segments lead to more effective and resonant content?
3. What are the most critical data points to collect when building an audience profile for content marketing?
4. Provide a step-by-step process for validating audience assumptions through interviews and research.
5. Show examples of how the same core topic can be angled differently for three distinct audience segments (e.g., a CEO, a technical manager, and an end-user).

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to move beyond basic personas to build a complete audience architecture. The video must cover the key components of a detailed audience profile and provide a clear, actionable framework for identifying, profiling, and validating at least three primary audience segments.
