## Source Generation Prompts

1. What is the "Hub and Spoke" model of internal linking and why is it so effective for SEO and user experience?
2. What are the key rules for creating effective, contextual internal links between content pieces?
3. How does a dense web of internal links help to signal topical authority to search engines?
4. What is the process for conducting an "orphan page" audit to find and integrate disconnected content?

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to build a powerful content ecosystem through a strategic internal linking. The video must visually explain the "Hub and Spoke" model, provide clear rules for creating effective contextual links, and offer a simple process for mapping out the connections between your pillar, cluster, and supporting content.
