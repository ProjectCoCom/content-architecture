# Video Source for: Content Format Selection

## Source Generation Prompts

1. How does choosing the right content format increase engagement, improve comprehension, and make content more shareable?
2. What is a "Content Repurposing" strategy and how does it maximize the ROI of major content investments?
3. Provide a framework for matching the right content format to the right strategic goal (e.g., SEO vs. lead generation).
4. What is a "10x Content Asset" and what are some examples of high-value, interactive formats that can change a brand's market position?
5. Show examples of how a single topic can be effectively presented in multiple formats (e.g., a blog post, a video, an infographic, and a checklist).

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to choose the right content format for the right audience and goal. The video must provide a clear framework for matching formats to strategic goals and show a practical example of how a single piece of research can be repurposed into a variety of content formats to maximize its reach and impact.
