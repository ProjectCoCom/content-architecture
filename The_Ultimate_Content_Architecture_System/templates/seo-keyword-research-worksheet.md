### **Column Breakdown**

*   **Keyword**: The specific keyword phrase you are targeting.
*   **Search Volume**: The estimated monthly search volume for the keyword (use a tool like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner).
*   **Keyword Difficulty**: A score (usually out of 100) that estimates how difficult it is to rank for the keyword (use a tool like Ahrefs or SEMrush).
*   **CPC (USD)**: The estimated cost-per-click for the keyword in Google Ads. This can be a good indicator of commercial intent.
*   **Intent**: The likely intent behind the search (Informational, Commercial, Navigational, Transactional).
*   **Priority**: Your priority for targeting this keyword (High, Medium, Low).
*   **Notes**: Any other relevant information, such as the type of content you could create for this keyword, or any SERP features you've observed.
