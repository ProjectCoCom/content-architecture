# Outreach and Promotion Email Templates

## 1. Expert Interview Request

**Subject:** Collaboration with [Your Brand] - [Your Topic]

Hi [Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I'm the [Your Title] at [Your Brand].

I'm a big admirer of your work on [Their Topic], especially your recent [article/podcast/etc.]. Your insights on [Specific Point] were particularly interesting.

We are creating a piece of content on [Your Topic] and I was hoping to include your expert perspective. Would you be open to a brief 15-minute chat sometime next week?

Thanks for your time and consideration.

Best,

[Your Name]

## 2. Guest Post Pitch

Hi [Name],

Best,

[Your Name]
