# Content Brief Template

## Instructions

**Purpose**: This template is designed to provide a clear and comprehensive blueprint for every article you create. Fill it out before you start writing to ensure your content is aligned with your strategy, optimized for search, and meets the needs of your target audience.

**How to Use (Pro Tier - Interactive)**: This is a Markdown file. You can copy and paste the raw text into a new `.md` file for each new piece of content. Alternatively, you can use a Markdown editor like Typora or Obsidian to work with it directly. You can also copy this into a Google Doc or Word document.
