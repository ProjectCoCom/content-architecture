# Quality Checklist

## Instructions

**Purpose**: This checklist is designed to ensure that every piece of content meets your quality standards before it is published. Go through this checklist for every article during the final review stage.

**How to Use (Pro Tier - Interactive)**: This is a Markdown file. You can use this as a reference, or copy and paste it into your project management tool, a Google Doc, or a new Markdown file for each article to track your progress.

---

## Pre-Publication Quality Checklist

### **Article**: [Title of Article]

### **1. Strategy & SEO**

*   [ ] Does the article align with the goals outlined in the content brief?
*   [ ] Is the primary keyword used in the title, URL, and introduction?
*   [ ] Are the primary and secondary keywords used naturally throughout the text?
*   [ ] Is the meta description compelling and within the character limit?
*   [ ] Are all internal and external links working correctly?
*   [ ] Does the article link back to its parent cluster and pillar pages?

### **2. Content & Readability**

*   [ ] Is the headline clear, compelling, and engaging?
*   [ ] Is the introduction hook effective and does it set clear expectations?
*   [ ] Is the content accurate, well-researched, and up-to-date?
*   [ ] Is the tone of voice consistent with our brand guidelines?
*   [ ] Is the article free of spelling and grammar errors? (Use a tool like Grammarly)
*   [ ] Is the formatting easy to scan? (short paragraphs, headings, lists)

### **3. Visuals & Media**

*   [ ] Are there relevant, high-quality images included?
*   [ ] Are all images optimized for web (compressed)?
*   [ ] Do all images have descriptive alt text?
*   [ ] If a video or other media is included, is it embedded correctly?

### **4. Conversion & User Experience**

*   [ ] Is there a clear and relevant call-to-action (CTA)?
*   [ ] Is the CTA appropriate for the user's stage in the journey?
*   [ ] Is the article easy to navigate on both desktop and mobile?
