# Video Source for: Industry-Specific Content Mapping

## Source Generation Prompts

1. Why is creating industry-specific content crucial for building authority and increasing relevance?
2. What is the process for strategically prioritizing which industry verticals to target with your content?
3. How can a business effectively research and understand the unique language, challenges, and regulations of a specific industry?
4. Provide a framework for outlining and creating a comprehensive "industry hub" page that serves as a central resource for a vertical.
5. Show examples of how to adapt a core piece of content (like a pillar page) to be hyper-relevant for a specific industry audience.

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to adapt a content strategy for specific industry verticals. The video must cover the process of prioritizing target industries, conducting in-depth industry research, and outlining a comprehensive "industry hub" page that speaks directly to the unique needs and language of that vertical.
