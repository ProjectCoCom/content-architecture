# Video Source for: Stakeholder-Focused Content

## Source Generation Prompts

1. Why is it critical to create content for every member of the buying committee, not just the primary decision-maker?
2. What is the process for mapping a buying committee and grouping various job titles into core stakeholder personas?
3. How do you create a detailed profile for a stakeholder persona that includes their unique goals, fears, and success metrics?
4. Provide a framework for brainstorming targeted content ideas that speak directly to the concerns of different stakeholders (e.g., CFO vs. IT Manager).
5. Show an example of a "Content for Champions" kit that equips an internal advocate to build a business case for your solution.

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to create content that persuades every member of a buying committee. The video must cover the process of mapping the committee, profiling each stakeholder's unique motivations, and brainstorming targeted content ideas that address the specific questions and concerns of each role.
