# Video Source for: Supporting Content Network

## Source Generation Prompts

1. What is the role of supporting content in capturing long-tail keyword traffic and achieving topical dominance?
2. What are the best methods for systematically identifying and brainstorming ideas for supporting content?
3. How do you use Google's "People Also Ask" and "Related Searches" features to generate an endless list of supporting content topics?
4. Provide examples of different supporting content formats, such as "How to X," "What is Y," and "X vs. Y."
5. What is the ideal structure for a supporting content article to ensure it provides a quick, direct, and valuable answer to a user's question?

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that explains how to build a supporting content network to achieve topical dominance. The video must explain how to use Google search features to brainstorm and prioritize a list of specific, question-based article ideas, and provide a clear template for outlining a focused and effective supporting content piece.
