# Video Source for: Team & Resource Assessment

## Source Generation Prompts

1. What are the key roles and responsibilities in a scalable content marketing team?
2. How can a business conduct a skills inventory to honestly assess its in-house content capabilities?
3. Provide a framework for creating a content marketing budget that includes headcount, tools, and promotion.
4. What is a "hybrid" team model and how does it combine in-house strategists with a flexible network of freelancers?
5. What are the key components of a "Content Operations Playbook" that allows a team to scale without sacrificing quality?

## Video Overview Directive

Directive: Create a video overview that guides viewers through the process of assessing their resources to ensure their content strategy is realistic and achievable. The video must cover how to perform a skills inventory, how to estimate the time commitment required by a content plan, and how to create a "first hire" plan to fill the most critical resource gaps.
